# Week 2 Concept — Missingness is a Mechanism, Not Noise

A blank cell still contains information.

The central claim of Week 2 is that missingness changes what can be inferred.
Rates alone are not enough. The mechanism matters:

- **MCAR** — missing completely at random
- **MAR** — missing at random, conditional on observed variables
- **MNAR** — missing not at random

RATEM therefore treats missingness as a first-class artifact rather than a hidden preprocessing step.
